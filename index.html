About Us

Brew & Go. Coffee is a local Kuching-based brand specializing in handcrafted coffee beverages. In addition to coffee, their menu also includes non-coffee beverages such as matcha and chocolate. Established in 2023 as a home-based business, Brew & Go opened its first coffee kiosk at One Jaya Mall in 2024. Recently, they expanded and launched their second kiosk at Plaza Merdeka Shopping Centre and are now seeking opportunities for further growth.

At Brew & Go, we are passionate about crafting the perfect cup of coffee. Our mission is to provide you with a unique and delightful coffee experience that you can enjoy on the go.

Whether you're in a hurry or looking to relax, our expertly brewed beverages are designed to satisfy your cravings.

Join us at our locations and discover the world of Brew & Go!
